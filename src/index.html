Chicago Free-Food Network Database

Designed a relational SQL database in Access to support a mock nonprofit during a SNAP benefits pause. The system tracks donors, food inventory, recipients, and distribution events, while allowing community members to submit local food resources. Built with usability in mind for staff and volunteers, and includes reporting tools for grant tracking and community analysis.
